21+1 American Poets Today
(A bilingual edition in two volumes, 312 + 312 p., Delta Press, 1986)

An anthology, selected by Emmanuel Hocquard and Claude Royet-Journoud, gathering twenty one American poets and one English poet (Tom Raworth whose books are published in the United States). The corresponding French volume 21+1 poètes américains d'aujourd'hui was translated by Marc Chénetier, Philippe Jaworski, and Claude Richard.
