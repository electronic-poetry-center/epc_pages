Tour Notes


     Must write memo to Omem Tsum: don't incur any expenses in Rucni.

     *

     Tragedy at Denword: Mort Sleam drowned in the Ydegrat

     Maelstrom.

     *

     Krap park: Nobody liked the name except Ydobon, Dekil, & Eman.

     *

     Looking at the Somen was Nemo's idea.

     *

     Skip Piks: it is as overrated as Detarrevo.

     *

     What Dottie Eittod had written on the Eton note was FUCK KCUF.

     "Who or what is Kay Cee You Ef?" asked young Deksa Gnouy.

     *

     Stopped at Deppots, ate the world famous Daerb bread, drank

     Reebknard beer. Does anybody know why it is called that?

     *

     Dear Enil Em (aka Daeh) read me the terrible headline: NO ACCORD

     ON DROCCA. What's next? El Birret, Txen, Stahw?

     *

     Otta Omirp in "Atto Primo": one more mistake of Ekatsim Eromeno.

     *

     On to Noitulos where we expect to find the solution to the Amgine

     enigma.

     *

     At Ta-Deyats Edna and E stayed at Hotel Letoh (where De Wercs

     screwed Olivia Aivilo).

     *

     Going up Rewot Tower, Senhor Rohnes Dias said to Deb Moor: "Is

     room in my bed? Si!

     *

     Evening at Gnineve. What's here besides the ruins of Ereh

     Sediseb?

     *

     Everyone is going to scream if we go one more time to Maercs or

     Emiter-Omeno.

     *

     Rerouted detourer.

OF BABAR

     

     

     i

     

     graven images of babar

     in the apartments of babar

     have a future and babar

     is spherical like pascal

     

     ii

     

     at the beginning

     mother & father

     created babar

     & all the stars

     

     iii

     

     the dancer can't step twice

     into the admirable

     very admirable

     booties of the great babar

     

     iv

     

     plus babar  minus babar

     do not say such oppositions

     can be swung to give a semblance

     sweetened wry pragmatic semblance

     of an instance of babar

     

     v

     

     the only way forward

     as it now appears

     is through misty babar

     o seductive illusion

     of the absence

     of the loving

     babar
