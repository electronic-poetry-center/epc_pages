from "Days"

by Hank Lazer

Hank Lazer's selections from _Days_ consists in a series of visual poems. Drawing on the expressivity of poems in manuscript (cf. Emily Dickinson fasicles), he melds standard typographic form with the emendations and variants typical of the editor's pen.

The following poems from the series are displayed as images so as to render their visual qualities as fully as possible. The medium resolution or mid-shot version is recommended for normal viewing; close-up and long shot are also available. The long shot versions sacrifice some resolution but minimize processing time.
