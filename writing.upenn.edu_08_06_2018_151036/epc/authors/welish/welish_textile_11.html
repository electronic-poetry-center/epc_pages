Because, then
                          if, then, then even as
                                                              such as in voice-over.


" 'because,' Schubert writes …"
                                               " 'Nevertheless, your life / seems to me wide with potential because of your / . . .' "


"because" pageant:
                                  to give voice to a cause
                                                                           choir

to ascertain causes beyond rhetoric
                                                           chorus.

Causality in lyric is trying.
                                          A song, a song beyond rhetoric
                                                                                              ours

insofar as . . .
                         let us each in turn declare

causes
               the disenchantment to which we write cheques
