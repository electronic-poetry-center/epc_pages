I must have played the game as well to attract these characters, Satan the Devil as an over-wrought "I'm so evil because I worship Satan" caricature. If we could kill the Devil personæ then maybe there'd be less annoying teenagers & self-idolizing rebels out there, that comfortable fleshy mound on which you sit (on which you are probably sitting RIGHT NOW!). That jolly round fat-cushion on top of which most North Americans spend the bulk of their time. Thus I dedicate the following œuvre, "There is no difference between high and low.
