Day Book of a Virtual Poet
Robert Creeley


The Day Book of a Virtual Poet provides a unique entrance into the ideas and practices - into the life, finally - of one of our great writers. This journal is the result of an extraordinary exchange between Creeley and a group of youngsters who participated in the adventure of an "on-line" poetry course. The teacher here is remarkably candid, while what he has to say is inflected by his natural elegance.

-- Burt Kimmelman
