Cuchutexto
Texts change, listless
Words change, cannily, as
language unfurls. Titles
of each language-block
are family, not foreign.

Loss Pequeño Glazier
Letters across the lintel
Convey you to screens
of text. As you linger by
waiting, you read language
layers familiar, not foreign.
