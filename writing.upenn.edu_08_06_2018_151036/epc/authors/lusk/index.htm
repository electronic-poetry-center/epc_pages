Bio


Born in Deep River, Ontario and raised in Ottawa, Dorothy Trujillo Lusk emigrated to the West Coast of Canada in 1974 and studied printmaking and painting at Camosun College and Vancouver School of Art. She is associated with Red Queen, Kootenay School of Writing and Pacific Institute for Language and Literacy Studies.
