Crayon: Fetschrift for Jackson Mac Low's 75th Birthday


75th Birthday Festschrift for Jackson Mac Low: poet, playwright, painter, multimedia performance artist, a founding participant in Fluxus, and composer of music, performance works, and radio works.

Contributions include essays, poetry, graphics, photographs, an interview, and sixty minutes of music on a CD (319 pages) from a range of contributors. Crayon 1 is now available.
