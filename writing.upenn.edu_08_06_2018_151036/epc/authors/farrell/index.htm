Bio


Dan was born in Fort William, Ontario, in 1963, and grew up in British Columbia. From 1981 to 1996 his home was Vancouver where he worked as a letter carrier, cook and poet. In 1996 he emigrated to the US, living in Boston, Brooklyn, San Francisco and Los Angeles. In 2007 he completed a Masters of Archival Studies at UBC, and now works as a Records Analyst in Metro Vancouver.
