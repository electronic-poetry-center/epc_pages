Some of These Daze (on/around 9/11):

The first three of the pieces collected in this series were originally published on the Poetics List:

  • "It's 8:23 in New York"
  • "Today Is the Next Day of the Rest of Your Life"
  • "Aftershock"

These pieces were included in a special section of boundary 2, entitled "Memoranda on 9/11/01," edited by Paul Bové (29:1; Spring 2002). Thanks also to Meg Harvan for her work in getting this section out so quickly.

Report from  Liberty Street" was first written for the web site of the University of Chicago Press. It was subsequently published in 110 Stories: New York Writes After September 11, ed. Ulrich Baer (New York: NYU Press. 2002)

Mimi Gross and I collaborated on a book that used some of the writing from these pieces. Some of These Daze was published in 2005 by Granary Books:
