Bio



Lissa Wolsak lives and works in Vancouver, B.C., as an energy~field therapist and goldsmith. Due out in Fall 2008 is Squeezed Light: Collected Works 1995 ~ 2007, from Station Hill Press, Barrytown. Also forthcoming are several long-poem sequences entitled: p)light; eyeblight; LIGHTSAIL; Thrall; Of Beings Alone; Kairos; and Weilding Time.
