Product

The Obfuscator creates a new poem from your old poem by a simple yet elegant process: it takes each word, and finds longer words that contain the original word in their constituent letters, giving you the option of which to choose. For instance, if you obfuscate the word cat, the Obfuscator gives you the option of choosing from chat, cardinality, exacerbating, incarnation, socialist, vilification, and witchcraft. The Obfuscator takes just three easy steps:

  1. Enter poem for processing.
  2. Select new words from the lists provided.
  3. Read and save the obfuscated poem to a file.

Without losing the words of the original poem, The Obfuscator creates--as if by magic--a new more complex poem through the power of obfuscation.
