NO FRUCTOSE IN GOOSELAND
by Edwin Torres

The fruits of the child shall get
stuck in the labor the child
gets stuck during labor doing labor
the child obeys labor laws while enjoying
fruit the flaws for fruit are different
than the laws of children they occupy
a different obeying fruit obey chicken without laws
but the law maintains another standard for children
who labor under the fruit of their obeying
the child obeys when it's time for pain
nothing to do for the fruit of labor but obey
the child of the newly born pain.
