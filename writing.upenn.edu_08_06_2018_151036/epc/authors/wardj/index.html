Jay Ward

Jay Ward is a poet and performer who focuses on the spoken word. He is the frequent winner of poetry slams in Buffalo and has performed at the Nuyorican Poets Cafe and the Knitting Factory in New York City. Jay conducts regular writing and performing workshops in Western New York and coordinates the Spot Cafe / Borders reading series for the just buffalo literary center.
