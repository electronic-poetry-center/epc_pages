Martin Spinelli

Martin Spinelli is currently Senior Lecturer in Media and Film Studies at the University of Sussex. He is the producer of the series Radio Radio and LINEbreak as well as several other radio arts and literature programs, and has authored numerous essays on media poetry, aesthetics, history and politics in journals such as Postmodern Culture, The International Journal of Cultural Studies, and Convergence in addition to numerous anthology chapters. He is currently at work on a book about the history of literary experimentation on radio called The Mediated Word.
