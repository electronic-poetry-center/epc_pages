two from no truck

pseudo slammer
here, a botched cozy live-in. hankering, i "cause" a delay in the
system start-up. poised with an empty pistol in latent space.
more or less fain, error's habitual day parade inaugurates a
fuzzy death. funny, bursting the dove's eye, & lifers balk at the
riddle of breakage. what was i thinking, trying to blow the
cover off now. a surge of instrumentality comes all
grammatical & sexless in waves of declension & honeytones.
insidious. thusness can't get the letters off the fickle brain for
obsessive time rattling the bars of an emotion you could really
starve your way into. a photograph of melting snow, for
example.
