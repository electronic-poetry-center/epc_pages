Erin Wilson

 

Dear Ann

Cloudrise is sunrise during waking, the sun was falling toward you

 

The recurring memory

is not of windows

although I painted a chest that color green

but of a stainless steel bowl between your mother's knees. She was pitting elderberries for a pie.

In remembering there is a moment of believing: these words, this letter
