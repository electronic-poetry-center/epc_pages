Clark Lunberry & David Reisman

 

David Reisman is an artist and writer who lives in New York. He is also Senior Editor in the Education Publishing Department of Channel Thirteen, the nation's flagship public television station. He exhibits his art work at Pierogi 2000, an innovative gallery in Brooklyn, New York. In June, 2000, his drawings were featured in a group exhibition called "In your Dreams" at FA 1 contemporary Art in London.

Clark Lunberry is a writer and visual artist who lives in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Currently a doctoral candidate in the Modern Studies Program at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee, Lunbery's work has appeared in Chicago Review, Discourse, The2River View, Kyoto Journal, Comparative Literature, Sugar Mule and LVNG. Also, a book of his poetry and photography, StonePoems, was published in 1999 from Kalligram Press, Bratislava, Slovakia.
