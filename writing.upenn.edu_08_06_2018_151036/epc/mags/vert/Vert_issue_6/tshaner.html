Tim Shaner

ad-verse

wendy's my all new pathfinder
vicks schwinn poupon amway farms
the frisky grey christian bug raid kills
everready certs dior manwich squirt
the hickory maxwell footaction housed
whips jolly cool irish ranchers into spring

 

Tim Shaner is a doctoral candidate in UB's Poetics Program, he is at the beginning stage of of his dissertation, which is on poetry and work. With Kristen Gallagher, he edits, curates Rust Talks. Last year the poetics journal verdure published "Word: Letter: World - a Festschrift for Robert Grenier," which he co-edited with Michael Rozendal. Tim Shaner assists Chris Alexander moderating the Poetics Listserv.
