The Philosopher-Kings™

They negotiate complex matters
complexly.
They talk rapidly, act decisively,
do many things at once.
They do not hesitate, or
doubt themselves. They do not
flinch at criticism. They intend
to win. They set the bar
higher all the time, they
attempt more difficult, more
complex, more ambitious things.
Their goal is always to
make more things possible, to
affect the lives of more people,
to influence more people with
their creations, and to benefit
by that influence.
