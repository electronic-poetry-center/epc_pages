Larry Eigner,
             wants me to know
                                       he's from Swampscott
             heard me read
                                       Poet Visits Salem in October
             I know Larry
                                       I know yr stuff
             yr poetry appeared
                              in Child Life
                                               in 1937
             and a letter in 1939
                                      Good Citizenship on Halloween
                       I lifted that title
                                      on Independence Day
                       another light-fingered homage

             interrogate Tom Clark
                     in the church basement
                             he tries to answer you
             make small noises during Barbara Guest
                       in the neighborhood library
                              All Saints' Day last year
                                       Todos Santos in the back room
             elucidate Charles Olson
                      with yr inpenetrable light
                                Creeley stops to listen
                                        in the alumni lounge

             Larry Eigner applied
                       to the museum's facade
             words as diaphanous
                                        as the twilight
             Larry Eigner on tv
                      my tape runneth out
             I'll have to memorize
                     yr intuitive typewriter

             Larry, the rain today
                                      in Berkeley
                       is falling
                       straight
                       down
                                on tumbling wisteria
             under the eaves
                               a copper spider
                               frets his web
                                                               in mid-air
             

                                                         1 APR 96
                                                          (spontaneous poem)
             

                                                                                    —Stephen Ronan
