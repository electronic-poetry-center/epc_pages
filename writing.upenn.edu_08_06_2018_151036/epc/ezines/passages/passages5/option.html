Ode to Odessa
         

          Too odd ode
          said ode sad
          toad add odd
          sod sat to ate
          his gills were problematic
          this used to be a different place
          made of Saturday pirogies
          and big pigs and
          cows now on she
          snow blower, add
          flown as a sound
          meower second
          hour flip through
          not go out with rising
          horns which Vlad
          will cherish & love
          when I started growing blonde
          as a soldier
          amputated by war
          unwanted & friendless
          cherishes his last
          remaining leg
          I cherish what passes
          lovingly from my hands
          effortlessly
         

                 —Lee Ann Brown, Laynie Browne, Douglas Rothschild,
                Anselm Berrigan, Lisa Jarnot, Bill Luoma, Drew Gardner
                April 2, 1997   first poem of the Optionists
