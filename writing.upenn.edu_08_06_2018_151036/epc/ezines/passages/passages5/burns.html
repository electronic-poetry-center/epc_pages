relieved by honeysuckle

            the plane echo passes

            boards being tossed

            dogs bark

            annoyingly a gnat ear close

                                -

            against white grey fog

            birds scribbling

            dots  & gyres

            translate

            the porous tablet
           
                                -

            articulate cloud

            a plane crosses silent

            finches tighten the distance

            another moment

            settles the same again

            mimetic logic sways a breeze

            unaccountable that leaf

            holding its own

            the green, brown

            (as if it were a tree)

                                 -

            space

            invokes the black and stars

            but it could be the horizon

            the long flat of blue

            we sometimes call the sea

                                 -

            it's as if the miles along the road meant something

            what exactly is your guess

            trees running into each other 70 m.p.h.

                                 -

            finger of wind

            (humanity of nature, more evident)

            a blond hair rides along

            this trap

            turning of the memory wheel

                                -

            an apple blossom barely plucked

            a dog has to shit but can't

            taken together we get a scene

            forming as a painting might

            generate quiet through frenzy
           

                                                             —Avery E. D. Burns
