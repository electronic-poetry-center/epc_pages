FIND DICK
 by Mark Peters

Jane said, "I see you.
I see you, Sally.
I can find you.
I can not find Dick.
Help me, Sally.
Help me find Dick."

Jane said, "Oh, Father.
I can not find Dick.
And we can not play.
Help me, Father.
Help me find Dick."

Father said, "Look, Jane.
Look, look, look.
You can find Dick."

Sally said, "Oh, oh.
I see Dick now.
Father and I see Dick.
We see funny Dick.
Look, Jane, look.
You can find Dick now."

Pub. May 2001
