Sunset Debris

The drums of irony scavenge the nightmares of omniscience.
I wanted to say omnipotence's nightmares, but went with it.
What I mean is the cultural æffluvium processed by massed media.
What I mean is the social role many _____'s are taking on themselves.
"When their dream is the world's nightmare we will be there
        like vultures to shock them in their dream."
Until they harmonize the oneiric essence
or decide never to close eyes again.
