Tinfish From: Susan Schultz

TinFish, a journal of experimental poetry, with an emphasis on work from the Pacific region, welcomes contributions. Please write to the editor, Susan M. Schultz, at 1422A Dominis Street, Honolulu, HI 96822, or Dept of English, 1733 Donaghho Road, University of Hawaii, Honolulu, 96822. The first issue will be free to anyone who asks for a copy (and you can do this over email). Please spread the word. And many thanks to those who responded to my earlier query.
