Larry Eigner dedication issue for PASSAGES Date: Tue, 6 Feb 1996 14:07:09

* * * ANNOUNCEMENT * * *

The editors of the on-line poetics journal PASSAGES
are preparing an issue dedicated to Larry Eigner. We
are collecting remembrances, reflections, and corres-
pondences, personal or philosophical, by persons ac-
quainted with Larry's accomplishments and spirit.

We are soliciting tributes from non-email members of
the poetics community, and ask that you spread word of
our project to anyone who might be interested.

Contributions may be sent to PASSAGES c/o
